E-books, short for electronic books, are digital publications that are designed to be read on electronic devices such as computers, tablets, smartphones, or dedicated e-book readers. E-books can be downloaded from the internet, purchased from online retailers, or borrowed from libraries, and they come in various formats such as PDF, EPUB, MOBI, or AZW. E-books are typically created from a source document, such as a manuscript or a typeset document, and then formatted to fit the electronic device's screen size and resolution. E-books can contain text, images, videos, and other multimedia elements, and they offer a convenient and portable way to access information and entertainment.
